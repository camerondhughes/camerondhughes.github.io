Flag Quest

How to Play

Guess a country and with every guess you will receive a color of the flag in order from largest area to smallest. If colors have equal proportions, it is ordered from left to right, or top to bottom. You will be notified when you have all the colors. The greyscale image of the flag is your last opportunity to win, have fun!
